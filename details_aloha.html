A Website About the Moon

The Aloha Telescope project aims to bring astronomy to K-12 classrooms, reaching thousands of students and teachers. The program provides access to telescopic views of the moon and other celestial objects, expanding students' exposure to STEM. Aloha continues to enhance its curriculum with plans to extend its reach to more schools, including enabling daytime access to Georgia Tech telescopes for students in Hawaii. The initiative focuses on inspiring young minds through interactive astronomy education.
